Oh, thanks for reminding me!

My name is Ameen Basith, I live in Orange County, California, and I am currently studying Computer Science at San Diego State University.

I started my programming journey in the Fall of 2020, when I, as a Biology major, was tasked with completing a Biostatistics course carried out in 'R'. I had never heard of this language prior to this course, however, when I realized how much I enjoyed working in this environment, I knew I had to make an important decision as to my future as a Biology major.

I proceeded to take all the necessary courses to transition into becoming a Computer Science major, and, although incredibly difficult at first, I haven't looked back since then!

Ever since the Spring of 2021, I have been performing exceptionally in my courses, including Data Structures, where I earned a final grade of an A, as well as a GPA of 3.85/4.00

I am always ready to learn, and will never give up until my vision is fully functional and includes every creative aspect that I imagined.
